How to Use This Tracker in Notion

  1. Toggle between Year and Month views using the buttons
  2. Click on individual days to mark your progress (multiple clicks cycle through progress levels)
  3. Use the year selector to view different years (including future years)
  4. In month view, use the arrows to navigate between months
  5. Export your data using the Export Data button
  6. To embed in Notion, take a screenshot and add it as an image
