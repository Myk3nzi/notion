How to Use This Tracker in Notion

  1. Click on individual days to mark your progress (multiple clicks cycle through progress levels)
  2. Use the year selector to view different years
  3. Export your data using the Export Data button
  4. To embed in Notion, click the three dots in the top right corner of this page and select "Print", then save as PDF
  5. Alternatively, you can take a screenshot and add it to Notion as an image
  6. For advanced integration, you would need to use Notion's API (more complex setup)
